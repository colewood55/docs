About This Activity

The Burnout Budget is an experiential classroom simulation grounded in Conservation of Resources (COR) theory and Self-Determination Theory (SDT). Students allocate limited tokens across life domains, encounter fixed costs and unpredictable life events, and connect firsthand experience to the theory of stress, burnout, and resilience. Runs in 40–60 minutes. Adaptable for undergraduate, graduate, and professional audiences.
